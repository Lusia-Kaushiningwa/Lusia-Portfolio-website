Who Am I?

I am Lusia, a passionate beginner full-stack developer who enjoys turning ideas into functional and visually appealing web experiences.

My journey into tech started with curiosity about how websites work. That curiosity quickly grew into a passion for building them myself.

“Growth happens when curiosity meets consistency.”

I enjoy solving problems, learning new technologies, and constantly improving my skills. Every project I build represents progress in both coding and creativity.

How It All Started

My development journey began when I wanted to understand what happens behind the screen. What started as small HTML experiments quickly expanded into CSS layouts and JavaScript logic.

In the beginning I struggled with positioning, responsiveness and coding logic. But each bug I solved improved my problem-solving skills.

Instead of giving up, I learned to research, experiment and keep improving step by step.
